The test produces a 10ms audio signal in a loop firing a trigger signal at LPT1 every loop re-entry.
This test allows you to measure audio delay, -rise and -fall times.
The test lasts about 20 seconds and can be interupted by pressing the "P" key.
Pressing "R" wil resume the signal until 20 seconds of play are over.

The scenario uses the enter key as the only response button.
LPT1 is set as output port
